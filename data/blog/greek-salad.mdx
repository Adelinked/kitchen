## Ingredients

- 4 large tomatoes, diced
- 1 cucumber, peeled and diced
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
- 200g feta cheese, crumbled
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and black pepper to taste

## Directions

### Step 1

In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and crumbled feta cheese.

### Step 2

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.

### Step 3

Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all ingredients.

### Step 4

Chill in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes before serving.

### Step 5

Serve the Greek salad on individual plates and enjoy!
