## Ingredients

- 2 salmon fillets
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- 2 cups cooked jasmine rice
- 1 cup broccoli florets, steamed
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
- Sesame seeds for garnish
- Green onions, chopped, for garnish

## Directions

### Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

### Step 2

In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and sesame oil.

### Step 3

Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the teriyaki sauce over the salmon.

### Step 4

Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through.

### Step 5

In serving bowls, assemble cooked jasmine rice, steamed broccoli, julienned carrot, and sliced red bell pepper.

### Step 6

Place the baked teriyaki salmon on top.

### Step 7

Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
