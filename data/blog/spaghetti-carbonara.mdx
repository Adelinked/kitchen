## Ingredients

- 400g spaghetti
- 150g pancetta, diced
- 3 large eggs
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

## Directions

### Step 1

Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.

### Step 2

In a skillet, cook the diced pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.

### Step 3

In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper.

### Step 4

Combine the cooked spaghetti, crispy pancetta, and egg mixture in a large bowl. Toss until the pasta is evenly coated.

### Step 5

Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley.
