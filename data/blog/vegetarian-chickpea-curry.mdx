## Ingredients

- 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro for garnish
- Cooked basmati rice for serving

## Directions

### Step 1

In a large skillet, saut√© the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger until the onion is translucent.

### Step 2

Add the chickpeas, diced tomatoes, coconut milk, curry powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine.

### Step 3

Simmer the curry over medium heat for about 25-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the flavors meld and the curry thickens.

### Step 4

Serve the vegetarian chickpea curry over cooked basmati rice and garnish with fresh cilantro.
